Reviews

Lucia B. from Yelp

Best Thai food in CO hands down! After visiting Thailand I have to say I like the Pad Thai from Wild Ginger even better. Moved away from CO a few years ago, recently came back to visit and I was pleasantly surprised that the quality and flavors in this place are still the same. My go to dish is always Pad Thai, I haven’t had one as delicious. I love spicy food but I have to admit that Thai spices are another level so I always order mild and ask for a little bit of spice. The medium is sometimes good but sometimes too spicy, so play it safe with the mild. Must try their handmade coconut ice cream!!! It is absolutely delicious.

5.0 star rating 7/13/2017
Ruthanne R. from Yelp

This was SO GOOD. I have missed good Thai food so badly since we left Seattle. Visiting CO, we decided to try Wild Ginger, and wow, were we blown away! The flavors and presentation are both amazing. If you want a fantastic Thai meal, this is the place to go.
